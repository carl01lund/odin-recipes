Strawberry Soup IV

Description

This is a very nice soup to eat during hot summer days. The flavor of the wine in this soup is important, so be sure to use a good one.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • â…“ cup white sugar
  • 2 cups chopped strawberries
  • 1 cup orange juice

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan mix wine and sugar, and bring to a boil. Let boil for 5 minutes and then remove from heat. Let cool.
  2. Puree strawberries and wine mixture in a blender or food processor. Blend until very smooth and then stir in orange juice. Chill for at least 3 hours before serving.
